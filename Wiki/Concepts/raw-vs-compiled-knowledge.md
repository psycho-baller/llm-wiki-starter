# Raw Vs Compiled Knowledge

## Summary

Raw sources preserve original context. Compiled Wiki notes turn useful source-backed claims into concise, reusable knowledge.

## Why It Matters

- Raw notes keep evidence intact.
- Compiled notes reduce repeated broad-context reading.
- Source links keep compiled claims traceable back to the original material.
